ABOUT

Alexandra Cabrillos, founder and owner of Alexa's Closet, had the desire to show her vision to the world with her unique set of creativity. With love, devotion, and passion, her dream came to life hoping to spark the dreams of others. Everyone, regardless of one's gender, must be given the chance to show themselves with style from one's love creation. At Alexa's Closet, we provide you with quality clothing that suits not only the style of society but your comfortability. We strongly promote neutral colors that are easily paired with other accessories or accents to your outfit.
